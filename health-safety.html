Health and Safety

Ensuring well-being through responsible design and usage

DESCRIBE

Who?

Employees, users, and the general public who interact with technology and physical environments.

Where?

Health and safety considerations apply in workplaces, public spaces, and home environments.

What?

Implementing safety protocols, ergonomic designs, and protective measures reduces risks and promotes well-being.

Conclusion

Prioritizing health and safety minimizes injuries and enhances the quality of life for individuals.

EXPLAIN

Why?

Health and safety precautions protect individuals from harm and improve productivity.

How?

Using ergonomic chairs and proper lighting reduces strain and improves comfort in workplaces.
