Usability

Enhancing user experience and efficiency

DESCRIBE

Who?

Users of digital products, websites, and software who rely on intuitive and efficient interfaces.

Where?

Usability is crucial in websites, applications, devices, and any user-facing digital system.

What?

Usability measures how easy, efficient, and satisfying a system is for users to interact with.

Conclusion

Improving usability leads to better user experiences, increased engagement, and reduced frustration.

EXPLAIN

Why?

Good usability enhances productivity, reduces errors, and ensures users can complete tasks effectively.

How?

Using clear navigation, responsive design, and user-friendly interfaces improves usability.
