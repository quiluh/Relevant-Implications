Privacy

Protecting personal data and digital security

DESCRIBE

Who?

Individuals, businesses, and organizations that handle or store personal data.

Where?

Privacy concerns arise in social media, online transactions, workplace monitoring, and data storage.

What?

Privacy involves safeguarding personal data, ensuring secure communication, and preventing unauthorized access.

Conclusion

Maintaining privacy helps protect identities, prevent fraud, and build trust in digital and physical interactions.

EXPLAIN

Why?

Privacy is essential to prevent identity theft, unauthorized surveillance, and misuse of personal data.

How?

Using encryption, strong passwords, and privacy settings can help secure personal information.
