Functionality

Ensuring a system works as intended to meet user needs

DESCRIBE

Who?

Users who rely on technology, applications, or devices to perform specific tasks.

Where?

Functionality is crucial in software, websites, and hardware across industries, workplaces, and daily life.

What?

Ensuring that all features perform correctly, respond efficiently, and provide the expected outcomes.

Conclusion

Reliable functionality ensures a positive user experience, builds trust, and prevents frustration or inefficiency.

EXPLAIN

Why?

Reliable functionality ensures user satisfaction and trust in the system.

How?

Adding error handling mechanisms helps users recover from mistakes without losing progress.
