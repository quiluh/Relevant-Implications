Social Implications

Understanding the effects of technology on society

DESCRIBE

Who?

Individuals, communities, and societies affected by digital communication and social interactions.

Where?

Social implications occur in workplaces, schools, online platforms, and public spaces.

What?

Social implications involve the impact of technology on communication, relationships, and behavior.

Conclusion

Understanding social implications helps create ethical guidelines for responsible digital interactions.

EXPLAIN

Why?

Social considerations ensure technology promotes inclusivity, mental well-being, and positive engagement.

How?

Encouraging respectful communication, digital literacy, and community-building fosters positive social impact.
