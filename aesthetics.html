DESCRIBE

Who?

Users of websites, apps, or physical spaces often judge quality based on design.

Where?

A well-designed product enhances user experience, making it more engaging and enjoyable.

What?

Thoughtful use of colors, typography, and layout can make a system more intuitive and visually appealing.

Conclusion

Aesthetics influence user perception, engagement, and satisfaction, impacting both digital and physical environments.

EXPLAIN

Why?

How?
