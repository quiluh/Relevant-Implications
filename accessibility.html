Accessibility

Ensure your product is usable by a various population

DESCRIBE

Who?

Many people have disabilities, such as visual impairments, hearing loss, or mobility issues

Where?

These individuals need to access websites, software, and physical spaces in their daily lives, whether at work, home, or in public places

What?

Implementing accessibility features, such as screen reader compatibility, captions, and wheelchair-friendly designs, ensures inclusivity

Conclusion

Accessibility ensures that everyone, regardless of ability, can fully participate in and benefit from technology, services, and infrastructure.

EXPLAIN

Why?

Ensuring accessibility allows a wider audience to use technology, increasing inclusivity and usability.

How?

By using appropriate color schemes, we can make a website more visible for people who are color blind.
